What are your check-in and check-out times?

Check-in time: 2:00 PM - 11:00 PM

Check-out time: 7:00 AM - 11:00 AM

Early check-in and late check-out may be available upon request and are subject to availability. Additional charges may apply for late check-out after 11:00 AM.

How can I make a reservation?

You can make a reservation through several methods:

  • Online: Use our secure booking system on our website
  • Phone: Call our reservations team at +1 (234) 567-8901
  • Email: Send your request to reservations@sunsethostel.com
  • In person: Visit our reception (24/7)

We recommend booking in advance, especially during peak seasons.

What types of rooms do you offer?

We offer four types of accommodations:

  • 6-Bed Dormitory: Perfect for budget travelers ($25/night)
  • 4-Bed Dormitory: Less crowded option ($30/night)
  • Private Single Room: With private bathroom ($50/night)
  • Private Double Room: For couples or friends ($70/night)

All rooms include free WiFi, lockers, and linens. Private rooms also include towels and toiletries.

What facilities are available at the hostel?

Our facilities include:

  • Common Areas: Lounge, TV room, study room
  • Kitchen: Fully equipped with appliances and utensils
  • Laundry: Self-service washers and dryers (coin-operated)
  • Bathrooms: Clean, shared facilities with private showers
  • Outdoor: Terrace and garden area
  • Services: 24/7 reception, luggage storage, tour desk
  • Amenities: Free high-speed WiFi throughout

What is your cancellation policy?

Our cancellation policy varies based on the rate plan:

  • Flexible Rate: Free cancellation up to 24 hours before check-in
  • Standard Rate: Free cancellation up to 48 hours before check-in
  • Non-refundable Rate: No cancellation or changes allowed

No-shows and early departures are charged the full amount. Please check your booking confirmation for specific cancellation terms.

How do I get to Sunset Hostel from the airport?

There are several options to reach us from the airport:

  • Airport Shuttle: We offer airport transfer service for $25 (book in advance)
  • Taxi: Approximately $40-50, 30-40 minutes
  • Public Transport: Take the airport express bus to downtown, then metro line 2 to Central Station (total $8, 60 minutes)
  • Ride-share: Uber/Lyft available for $35-45

Our address: 123 Sunset Boulevard, Central District, City 12345

Is WiFi available throughout the hostel?

Yes! We offer free high-speed WiFi throughout the entire hostel, including:

  • All guest rooms (dormitories and private rooms)
  • Common areas (lounge, TV room, study room)
  • Kitchen and dining area
  • Outdoor terrace
  • Reception area

The WiFi speed is optimized for work and streaming. Login details are provided at check-in.

What is your age policy?

Our age policy is as follows:

  • Minimum age: 18 years old to check-in without adult supervision
  • Youth (16-17): Must be accompanied by an adult or provide written parental consent
  • Children (under 16): Must be accompanied by an adult at all times
  • Family rooms: Available upon request for families with children

Age verification may be requested at check-in. Please contact us in advance if you have specific requirements.

Are linens and towels provided?

Yes, we provide the following:

  • All guests: Fresh linens (sheets, pillow, blanket) are provided for all beds
  • Dormitory guests: Towels are available for rent at $2 per stay
  • Private room guests: Towels and basic toiletries are provided free of charge
  • Additional: Extra blankets and pillows available upon request

All linens are professionally cleaned between guests. Lockers are available for storing personal items.

Do you have laundry facilities?

Yes, we have self-service laundry facilities available 24/7:

  • Washing machines: $3 per load (includes detergent)
  • Dryers: $2 per load (45 minutes)
  • Ironing: Free iron and ironing board available
  • Change machine: Available at reception for coins

Laundry soap is provided. The laundry room is located on the ground floor next to the kitchen.
