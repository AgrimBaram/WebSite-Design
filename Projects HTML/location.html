Step-by-Step Directions

From the Airport

1

Airport Express Bus

Take the Airport Express Bus (Line A1) to Central Station. Buses depart every 20 minutes.

2

Metro Connection

At Central Station, take Metro Line 2 towards Downtown. Get off at Sunset Station.

3

Final Walk

Exit the metro station at Gate B. Walk straight for 300m, turn right on Sunset Boulevard. We're on your left.
