Our House Rules

To ensure a comfortable stay for all guests, please read and follow our rules

Essential Rules

1

Check-in & Check-out

Check-in: 2:00 PM - 11:00 PM
Check-out: by 11:00 AM
Late check-out available upon request

2

Quiet Hours

Quiet hours: 10:00 PM - 7:00 AM
Please respect other guests
Use headphones in common areas

3

No Smoking

Strictly no smoking indoors
Designated smoking area outside
$200 fine for indoor smoking

4

Shoes Off

Remove shoes in dormitories
Use provided slippers
Keep floors clean for everyone

5

Security

Always lock your locker
Don't share access codes
Report suspicious activity

6

Cleanliness

Clean up after yourself
Use designated trash bins
Recycle when possible

Important Notice

Guests who violate house rules may be asked to leave immediately without refund. Repeated violations will result in being banned from the hostel.

Kitchen & Common Areas

  • Clean as you go: Wash your dishes immediately after use
  • Label your food: Use provided labels and containers
  • Share the space: Be mindful of others waiting to use facilities
  • Conserve energy: Turn off lights and appliances when not in use
  • Respect food: Don't consume food that isn't yours
  • Report issues: Inform staff of any maintenance problems

Prohibited Items & Activities

The following are strictly prohibited: Weapons of any kind, illegal substances, open flames/candles, pets (except service animals), excessive alcohol consumption, loud parties, and any form of harassment or discrimination.

Policies

Cancellation Policy

Free cancellation 48 hours before arrival. 50% charge for late cancellation. No refund for no-shows.

Lost Key Policy

$25 fee for lost room keys. $50 fee for lost key cards. Report immediately to reception.

Visitor Policy

Registered guests only in rooms. Visitors allowed in common areas until 10:00 PM only.

Age Policy

Minimum age: 18 years. Under 18 must be accompanied by parent/guardian in private rooms only.

By making a reservation, you agree to abide by all house rules and policies. Our staff is here to help - if you have any questions, please don't hesitate to ask!
