Our Story

How It All Began

Sunset Hostel started in 2010 as a modest 5-room guesthouse operated by John and Sarah Thompson. As passionate travelers themselves, they wanted to create a space that felt like home for fellow wanderers. Their vision was simple: provide clean, safe, and affordable accommodation with genuine hospitality.

The first location was a converted family home in the heart of the city. Despite limited resources, the Thompsons focused on what mattered most: comfortable beds, hot showers, and warm smiles. Word quickly spread among backpackers about this hidden gem that offered exceptional value.

By 2012, demand had grown so much that they expanded to 20 rooms. In 2015, they moved to their current flagship location on Sunset Boulevard - a beautifully restored heritage building that maintains its historical charm while offering modern amenities.

Today, Sunset Hostel operates three locations across the city, hosting over 15,000 guests annually from more than 120 countries. Despite our growth, we remain true to our founding principles: treat every guest like family, maintain impeccable standards, and create meaningful travel experiences.
